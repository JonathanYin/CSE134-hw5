About Me

Hi, I'm Jonathan Yin, a 3rd year undergraduate majoring in Mathematics and Computer Science at UCSD. I have experience working in team-oriented environments on projects involving game design, web development, 3D modeling/rendering, and more!

Throughout my academic career, I have honed my skills in object-oriented programming, computer architecture, web technologies, and operating systems. Working independently and as part of a team, I have experience with software development processes such as agile, as well as CI/CD tools. As an engineer, I aspire to develop solid software and long for a challenge that keeps me up at night. I aim to bring an open mind to learning the latest technologies and practices in my work.

I'm always curious to hear from new people about new thoughts, ideas, problems, projects or collaborations. If you have something on your mind, feel free to get in touch with me!
